Step 1: Choose a Simple Topic - Pick a subject you're familiar with, like your name, hobbies, or family.
Step 2: Write Key Points - Identify 3-4 important ideas to focus on, such as your family members or a favorite hobby.
Step 3: Create Short Sentences - Write simple and clear sentences to explain your key points.
Step 4: Use Flashcards - Write important words or phrases on flashcards and review them regularly.
Step 5: Practice Speaking Aloud - Say your sentences out loud, either in front of a mirror or recorded, to gain confidence.
Step 6: Use Visual Aids - Show pictures, such as family photos, to help explain your ideas more clearly.
Step 7: Rehearse with a Friend - Practice speaking with someone who can give you feedback and help you improve.
