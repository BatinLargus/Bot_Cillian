Step 1: Pick a Simple Topic - Choose a topic you're comfortable with, such as “my daily routine” or “a favorite vacation.”
Step 2: Outline Your Speech - Break it into 3 main sections: “morning routine,” “work/school,” and “evening plans.”
Step 3: Add Details - For each section, add 1-2 more sentences explaining what you do or how you feel.
Step 4: Practice Speaking - Try to speak without reading your notes too much, relying only on them as a guide.
Step 5: Use Transition Words - Practice using phrases like “First,” “Next,” and “Finally” to organize your speech.
Step 6: Get Feedback - Ask a friend or teacher to listen to your speech and give feedback for improvement.
Step 7: Record and Listen - Record yourself and listen back, noting areas for improvement in fluency and clarity.
